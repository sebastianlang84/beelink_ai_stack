## Summary
Hims & Hers is identified as a top investment pick for February 2026 following a 60% share price decline. The bull case rests on the company's ability to disrupt the $400 billion pharmaceutical industry through a unique healthcare platform model. Despite slowing growth rates, the company maintains high historical growth and improving operating margins. Expansion into longevity products and peptides is slated for 2026.

## Key Points & Insights
- **Market Disruption:** The company is not just a telehealth provider but is building a platform to disrupt the $400 billion pharmaceutical industry ("The real story about him and hers is about disruption. It's not about doing the same thing that everybody else is doing.").
- **Valuation Opportunity:** Shares have dropped over 60%, creating an entry point at an Enterprise Value (EV) to Sales ratio of 3.3.
- **Product Expansion:** Hims & Hers has launched labs and plans to enter the longevity market with peptides in 2026.
- **Competitor Landscape:** While competitor ROW has launched GLP-1 weight loss medications in pill form, Hims & Hers is argued to be a "much better deal for users" and more disruptive long-term despite lacking access to those specific pill forms.
- **Growth Outlook:** The analyst suggests that while growth is slowing from hyper-growth levels (70%+), maintaining a 20-25% growth rate is the typical trajectory for companies achieving 10x returns over a decade.
- **Operating Leverage:** The business is showing a trend of improving operating margins alongside revenue growth.
